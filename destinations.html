Bohol Island Tour from Cebu

Experience the natural wonders of Bohol on this full-day tour from Cebu. Visit the iconic Chocolate Hills, meet the adorable tarsiers, cruise along the Loboc River, and more!

Bohol Island Tour from Cebu

Experience Highlights

  • Visit the famous Chocolate Hills, Bohol's most iconic attraction
  • See the world's smallest primates, the Philippine tarsiers
  • Enjoy a buffet lunch on a floating restaurant along the Loboc River
  • Explore the historic Baclayon Church
  • Convenient round-trip ferry transfers included

What's Included

  • Round-trip ferry tickets
  • Air-conditioned vehicle transfers
  • English-speaking tour guide
  • Buffet lunch at Loboc River
  • All entrance fees

Itinerary

5:30 AM - Pick up from your hotel in Cebu City

6:30 AM - Ferry departure from Cebu to Bohol

8:00 AM - Arrival in Bohol, meet your guide

9:00 AM - Visit the Chocolate Hills

11:00 AM - Tarsier Conservation Area

12:30 PM - Loboc River Cruise with Lunch

2:30 PM - Baclayon Church and Museum

4:00 PM - Return to Tagbilaran Port

5:00 PM - Ferry back to Cebu

6:30 PM - Arrival in Cebu, transfer back to hotel
