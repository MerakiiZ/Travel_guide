Cebu Day Tour

Experience Highlights

  • ▪️ Start your day with a hearty breakfast at La Marea for delicious pastries and coffee, or at Cafe Laguna for classic Filipino breakfast dishes.
  • ▪️ Vitiy the iconic historical landmark where Ferdinand Magellan planted the cross that marked the arrival of Christianity in the Philippines in 1521.
  • ▪️ A must-visit just a short walk from Magellan's Cross, this historic church is the oldest Roman Catholic church in the Philippines, dating back to the 1500s.
  • ▪️ Visit this historical military defense structure, built by the Spanish in 1565. It's a great spot to learn more about Cebu's colonial past
  • ▪️ Head to the cool hills of Beverly Hills Subdivision to explore the colorful and serene Cebu Taoist Temple.
  • ▪️ No visit to Cebu is complete without tasting its famous lechon (roast pig). Head to Rico's Lechon for some of the best Cebu lechon, crispy and savory, paired with a flavorful dipping sauce.
  • ▪️ A short drive from the city, this vibrant flower farm in the highlands offers panoramic views of Cebu and colorful blooms. It's perfect for a scenic, Instagram-worthy stop.
  • ▪️ Head to Tops Lookout, one of Cebu's best spots to view the city. The view of Cebu City and the surrounding islands is breathtaking, especially in the late afternoon.
  • ▪️ For a mix of dining, shopping, and entertainment, head to Cebu IT Park or Ayala Center Cebu. Both places offer great dining options, shopping malls, and a lively atmosphere
  • ▪️ Enjoy a delicious Filipino dinner with a view. Lantaw offers great local dishes, or you can head to Marco Polo's Sky Lounge for a more upscale dining experience
