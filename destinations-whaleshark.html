Whale Shark + Sumilon Sandbar

Whale Shark + Sumilon Sandbar

What's Included

  • ✅ Pick up and drop off hotel in Cebu City / Mactan (we can pick up in other locations with additional surcharge)
  • ✅ Exclusive Air Conditioned Transportation
  • ✅ Local facilitator and guide
  • ✅ Light breakfast upon arrival in Oslob
  • ✅ Lunch
  • ✅ Boat ride and life vest
  • ✅ Snorkeling with the whale sharks for 30 minutes
  • ✅ Towel
  • ✅ Cool down at Tumalog Falls (Closed every second Wednesday of the month)
  • ✅ All entrance fees & snorkeling fees
  • ✅ Outrigger boat transportation to and from Sumilon island
  • ✅ Relaxing and snorkeling in Sumilon island sandbar
  • ❌ Motorbike down to and from Tumalog falls

Itinerary

4:00 AM - Pick up hotel

7:00 AM - Arrival in Oslob, light breakfast

7:30 AM - Whale shark watching or snorkeling

8:30 AM - Cool down at Tumalog Falls (Closed every second Wednesday of the month)

9:30 AM - Sumilon island sand bar

11:00 AM - Back to the mainland

12:00 PM - Lunch

1:00 PM - Depart back to the hotel

4:00 PM - Estimated return to hotel
